At 2 star a character either experiences an elemental awakening or they do not. The result is permanent and shapes everything that follows — class options, passive pools, and the character's elemental identity in combat.

---

## The Awakening

The awakening is a single roll at 2 star. Most characters awaken to a standard element. A small number awaken to light or dark. Some do not awaken at all and follow the physical path.

Awakening probability is influenced by race — certain races lean toward specific elements, others are broadly distributed, none are guaranteed. No race has a meaningful affinity for light or dark. LD awakenings are rare across the board.

The specific element awakened to is not fully player controlled. Race narrows the probability space, it does not fix the outcome.

---

## What It Unlocks

**Class path** — awakened characters gain access to mage and healer class options at 3 star alongside any physical options the weapon tree and early development would normally surface. A character can still choose a physical class at 3 star if one is presented the awakening opens elemental paths, it does not force them.

Light and dark awakenings guarantee a unique LD subclass at 3 star. Every standard physical class has an LD equivalent with a unique exclusive ability and access to LD specific passives. See [[Characters/Class/Class]].

**Elemental passive pool** — awakened characters gain access to the elemental passive pool for draws from 3 star onward. Non-awakened characters draw exclusively from the class pool. See [[Elemental Passives]].

**Built-in elemental passive** — at the moment of awakening the character gains a small passive specific to their element. This is not drawn from the passive pool and does not occupy a passive slot. (maybe?)

---

## Physical Path

Characters without an awakening develop entirely through physical class options. Their element exists as a matchup tag only — relevant for damage calculations and enemy weaknesses but not expressed in their active skills or passive pool. They draw exclusively from the class passive pool across all passive slots from 3 to 6 star, making them more narrowly focused within their role than an awakened character of the same class would be.
