**Resonance** as a buff that activates when 2+ bonded characters are alive in the party, lost when a bonded ally falls. Strength scales with how many bonds in the party. Mechanically reinforces the attachment philosophy — you're rewarded for raising characters together, not just for raw stats. Lives as a passive on certain class identities or as a leader trait variant.

**Vengeance Trigger** — when a bonded ally dies in combat, this character gains a significant buff for the rest of the fight. Different from generic ally-death triggers because it specifically responds to bonded loss, making the bond system have stakes in combat without being a chore to manage. Pairs cleanly with the Vengeful flat trait you already have. Could also be the mechanism that makes the Pallbearer trait actually feel meaningful.

**Bond Strike** — the reactive combat thing already in Loose Threads, tightened. Bonded characters have a chance to follow up on each other's kills with a basic attack. Bond strength scales the chance. This is the real Dual Attack equivalent for your game — Dual Attack rewards ATK investment, Bond Strike rewards roster investment. Probably stronger as a class-defining identity for a specific archetype than as a universal mechanic.
