Class skills fill the third and fourth active slots. They come from their class and cannot be changed under any circumstance.

---

## Slot Three — Unlocks at 3 Star

Unlocks at the same moment class locks in. Directly expresses the combat role.

- Warriors get an offensive cooldown skill
- Tanks get a taunt or damage mitigation skill
- Supports get a buff or heal
- Debuffers get a status effect skill

---

## Slot Four — Unlocks at 4 Star

Deepens the class identity established at slot three rather than introducing something new. Complements rather than duplicates.

- A warrior's second character skill might be an execute threshold ability
- A support's might be a group buff or cleanse
- A debuffer's might be a multi-target status effect

---

## Kit Completion

Five and six star add no new active skills. The kit is complete at 4 star. Late investment is about perfecting what exists, not expanding the toolkit.

Skills are not a power ladder from weakest to strongest. They are situational tools — which skill is correct depends on what is happening in the fight. Enemy count, HP thresholds, debuff states, turn order position. A well trained character in auto battle reads these conditions and responds. A poorly trained one uses whatever is off cooldown.
