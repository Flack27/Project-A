Elemental passives are available only to characters with an elemental awakening. They draw from a pool tied to the character's specific element, containing both minor and major passives. They do not replace class passives — both pools contribute across the passive slots from 3 to 6 star.

Characters without an awakening have no access to this pool.

Light and dark passives are part of this pool but are defined separately given the unique nature of LD archetypes — see [[Characters/Class/Class]].

---

## Pool Structure


Dark: 
more damage per debuff on enemy up to cap

Wind:
after enemy action increase turn meter by % (for all allies?)
