Elemental passives are available only to characters with an elemental awakening. They draw from a pool tied to the character's specific element, containing both minor and major passives. They do not replace class passives — both pools contribute across the passive slots from 3 to 6 star.

Characters without an awakening have no access to this pool.

Light and dark passives are part of this pool but are defined separately given the unique nature of LD archetypes — see [[Characters/Class/Class]].

---

## Pool Structure

Each element has its own pool of minor and major passives. Minor passives are small situational bonuses tied to the element's natural expression. Major passives meaningfully change how the element interacts with the rest of the kit or the enemy team.

_Full pools defined per element when the element list is finalised. Placeholder examples below._

**Fire** — minor: bonus damage against burning targets, chance to apply burn on basic attack / major: burn damage scales with ATK, skills deal bonus damage to targets already on fire

**Wind** — minor: SPD bonus when acting before all enemies, bonus damage against SPD debuffed targets / major: bar push magnitude increased, actions taken before enemies generate a stacking damage buff

**Earth** — minor: reduced damage from AoE, small DEF buff on taking a critical hit / major: shield generation scales with DEF, bonus damage on skills that also apply a DEF reduction
