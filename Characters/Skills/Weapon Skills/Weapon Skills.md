Weapon skills fill the first two active slots. Learned through a visible technique tree specific to each weapon type. Every weapon has 4 to 6 learnable techniques visible from the start, allowing players to plan development direction before committing.

Development speed is tied to growth rate and time spent using that weapon in combat. A high growth rate character develops techniques noticeably faster than a low growth rate character using the same weapon.

Techniques are fully interchangeable at any time for a small gold cost — these are trained skills, not innate abilities. Switching weapons before archetype locks resets progress toward current techniques and begins building toward the new weapon's tree instead, no penalty beyond time investment redirected.

---

## Slot Progression

Slot one is available from 1 star. A character starts with their basic weapon attack here and develops their first technique to replace it as they train. Slot two opens at 2 star.

---

## Influence on Class

Techniques developed before 3 star influence class options at the lock moment. A sword user who heavily developed defensive techniques gets tank and guardian more prominently in their class choices. One who went pure offensive gets warrior and assassin. Player decisions during early game actively shape what the character can become.

## Example Trees

_These are examples only. Final technique lists are defined per weapon type when weapon content is designed._

**Sword**

- Quick slash — fast low damage hit, high hit chance
- Heavy strike — slow high damage hit, chance to stun
- Defensive parry — reduces incoming damage for one turn
- Counter attack — triggers a free attack when this character is hit
- Wide slash — hits all enemies for reduced damage

**Bow**

- Aimed shot — single target, increased crit chance
- Rapid fire — two hits at reduced damage each
- Piercing arrow — hits through to a second target for partial damage
- Covering fire — reduces enemy SPD for one turn
- Pinning shot — chance to prevent target acting next turn

**Hammer**

- Crushing blow — high damage, chance to reduce DEF
- Ground slam — hits all enemies for reduced damage
- Shield bash — low damage, high chance to stun
- Fortify — increases own DEF for two turns
- Seismic strike — high damage, ignores a portion of enemy DEF
