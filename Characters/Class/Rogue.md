High crit burst dealer that operates from stealth and punishes kills with immediate follow-up actions. Stealth prevents direct targeting for a window, enabling setup before committing to the burst rotation. Crit rate and crit damage are the primary gearing targets — skills are designed around guaranteed or near-guaranteed crits on key hits rather than sustained output. Damage scales with missing HP on the target, rewarding target selection and finishing wounded enemies over raw aggression into full HP targets. Chain on kill grants an immediate extra action, turning a successful burst into a chain if conditions are right. AoE utility comes through buff steal and strip rather than damage spread — Rogue dismantles enemy buffed states rather than hitting everyone. Fragile and heavily punished if stealth is stripped or burst window is interrupted.
