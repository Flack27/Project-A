High ATK damage dealer that grows more dangerous as it absorbs punishment. Damage output scales with total HP lost during the fight — not current HP, but cumulative damage taken since the fight started. Low DEF means the scaling kicks in naturally without deliberate self-harm. Selfish and purely offensive, no team utility, no support presence. Everything points inward.

---

#### Passives

**Minor**

Rage — gains ATK Up for one turn each time this character is struck. Stacks with the HP lost scaling to reward characters that attract enemy attention.

Frenzy — basic attack hits twice when below 50% HP. Each hit generates character resource and applies on-hit effects separately.

Bloodlust — kills restore a flat amount of HP [placeholder]. Minor sustain on multi-enemy content, distinct from lifesteal as a build direction.

**Major**

Missing HP Scaling — gains a damage bonus for every [placeholder] HP lost during the fight, accumulating until death. Tracks cumulative damage taken, not current HP — healing does not reduce the stack. High HP investment raises the ceiling; low DEF accelerates reaching it.

Lifesteal — heals for [placeholder]% of all damage dealt. Sustains the berserker in the scaling range without external support. Does not reduce the missing HP stack since healing does not reverse damage taken.

Endure — survives one killing blow at 1 HP instead of dying. Resets after [placeholder] turns. Guarantees access to peak scaling once per cooldown without requiring a full party wipe to reach it.

Current HP Scaling — gains a damage bonus for every [placeholder] HP lost. High HP investment raises the ceiling.
