Damage dealer built around follow-up attacks and action economy disruption. Passive chance to follow up with a basic attack whenever an ally acts. Pinned as the primary debuff locks targets out of bar push effects, pairing naturally with the speed-dependent follow-up gameplan. Single target focus for priority kills, AoE volley option for pressure across the enemy party. Bonus damage when not hit the previous round rewards staying out of the frontline and punishes enemies who ignore the Ranger to focus other targets. Bow is the natural weapon but follow-up and pinned mechanics function identically on spear and claws — weapon choice changes technique expression, not class identity.
