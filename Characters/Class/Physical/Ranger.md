Damage dealer built around Mark and action economy disruption. Mark designates a priority target for the whole team — basic attacks deal bonus damage against it and any ally crit against it triggers a Ranger follow-up, once per turn. Pinned locks targets out of bar push effects, pairing naturally with a kit that rewards acting frequently. Bleed application and damage scaling with stack count provide sustained pressure alongside the burst potential of a fully marked target. Fragile and punished hard if caught — rewards staying out of the frontline and picking engagements carefully.

---

## Mark

Unique Ranger debuff. A Marked target takes bonus damage from basic attacks. When any ally lands a critical hit against a Marked target, Ranger follows up immediately with a basic attack. Follow-up triggers once per turn regardless of how many allies crit in that turn. only 1 mark can be active on a enemy at a time.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Volley** — AoE active. Strike all enemies. Each enemy has a chance to be applied [Mark]. Apply [Crit Rate+] to all allies.

**Hunter's Mark** — Single target active. High damage hit. High chance to apply [Mark] and [Pinned] to the target.

**Sentinel Call** — Active. Apply [Turn Meter+] and [Crit DMG+] to all allies.

**Predator** — Passive. Bonus damage against Bleeding targets, scaling with the number of Bleed stacks currently on them. All skills have a base chance to apply [Bleed] on hit.

**Flurry** — Active. Designate 3 basic attacks distributed freely across any enemies. Attacks can be focused on one target or spread. 

---

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Crippling Shot** — Basic attacks have a chance to apply a minor [Turn Meter-]to the target.

**Hunting Instinct** — At the start of each turn, apply [Mark] to the lowest HP enemy. If that enemy is already Marked, the existing [Mark] is refreshed.

**Exposed** — Bonus damage against [Pinned] targets.

**Finish Them** — Bonus damage against targets below X% HP. Threshold is higher than the execute effects on dagger and scythe techniques — more enemies fall within range, rewarding chasing wounded targets over clean executes.

**Stalker** — Weapon skills have a chance to apply [Mark] on hit.

**Marked for Death** — _Requires Stalker or Hunter's Mark to be drawn._ The bonus damage [Mark] applies to basic attacks is increased. Only appears in the minor draw pool if Stalker or Hunter's Mark already occupies a slot on this character.
