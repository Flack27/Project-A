DEF-scaling damage dealer with two viable build directions. Damage skills scale with DEF rather than ATK, making survival investment directly translate into offensive output. Crit rate is the multiplier — a crit-focused build at moderate DEF produces high damage spikes, a full DEF build produces a reliable damage floor that never goes away even without crits. Slow and deliberate, not a character that wins through action economy. Distinct from Guardian which sacrifices all damage for team protection — Warden is self-sufficient, threatening in its own right, and built to survive long enough for that DEF scaling to compound.
