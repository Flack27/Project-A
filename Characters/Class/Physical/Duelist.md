Single target damage dealer built around counter mechanics and escalating pressure on one enemy. Passive counter chance on incoming hits, with counters landing as guaranteed criticals. Single target skills hit the same target multiple times with each hit harder than the last, rewarding sustained focus over target switching. DEF penetration as a passive or late skill gives it a ceiling against high DEF targets that other pure ATK classes struggle with. Fragile — low DEF and HP means it loses badly to burst it cannot counter out of. Rewards players who manage incoming damage and pick fights it can survive long enough to punish.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Duelist's Stance** — Passive. Combat begins with [Counter] applied to self. Refreshes at the start of every other turn. The active version of this effect overrides remaining duration rather than extending it.

**Riposte** — Single target active. Always lands as a critical hit. Applies [Counter] to self for the duration. Provides the active path into the counter identity for characters who didn't draw the passive stance.

**Provoke** — Single target active. Always lands as a critical hit. Steals buffs and applies [Taunt] to the target for one turn. Forces the opponent's strongest attacker into the worst possible target choice.

**Last Bastion** — AoE active. Applies [Taunt] to all enemies and [Damage Reduction] to self for the duration. The single defensive turn available to the class, sized as a counter setup rather than full tanking.

**Pressure Point** — Single target active. Applies [Vulnerable] and [DEF-] to the target. Sets up the escalating-pressure gameplan against a priority enemy.

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

****

**Exploit Weakness** — Attacks against stunned or taunted targets always land as critical hits.

**Onslaught** — Multi-hit skills deal increasing damage on each successive hit against the same target within a single skill use.

**Piercing Edge** — Basic attacks ignore a portion of target DEF.

**Iron Resolve** — Damage taken from enemies this character has counter-attacked in the current round is reduced by a fixed percentage.

**Killing Counter** — Counter attacks always land as critical hits. (Requires Duelist Stance or Riposte)

**Follow Through** — The next active skill used after this character counters deals bonus damage. (Requires Duelist Stance or Riposte)
