All-rounder physical damage dealer with two distinct passive directions. The HP scaling passive trades crit entirely for a reliable damage floor that grows with max HP investment and holds up across long fights. The vulnerable passive amplifies the whole team's damage against priority targets while maximising Warrior's own output against them. Three active skills cover AoE clearing, debuff application and single target execute. Broadest weapon access of any physical class. No dramatic trade-offs, no hard counters — the all-rounder identity is the point.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**War Shout** — AoE active. Deal X% damage to all enemies. [Strip] one buff from all enemies. Apply [ATK-] and [Bleed] to all enemies.

**Finishing Blow** — Single target active. Multi-hit skill. The final hit deals bonus damage equal to a percentage of the target's missing HP. Damage scales with how low the target is — the closer to death, the harder the last hit lands.

**Blood and Iron** — Passive. At the start of combat, increase max HP by a percentage of total ATK. Each basic attack and skill hit deals additional damage equal to a percentage of max HP. This character cannot crit while this passive is active.

**Exploit** — Passive. All skills have a percentage chance to apply [Vulnerable] to their target. The effect of [Vulnerable] is increased by X% for damage dealt by this character.

**Press the Attack** — AoE active. Deal X% damage to all enemies. The main target takes bonus damage on top of the AoE hit.

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**From Strength** — Deal bonus damage while above X% HP.

**Battle Rhythm** — Every 2 turns, push all allies' action bars forward by X%.

**Surge** — Each skill use grants a stacking ATK buff. Stacks up to a fixed cap and persist until combat ends.

**Death Knell** — When any character dies, automatically trigger S1 weapon skill against the lowest HP enemy. Has an internal cooldown.

**Killing Spree** — When an enemy dies, reset own skill cd's
