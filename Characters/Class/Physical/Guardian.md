Active protection class that intercepts damage rather than just absorbing it. Damage redirect transfers a portion of incoming ally damage to this character, functioning as a genuine step-in-front mechanic rather than passive aggro. Taunt forces enemy attention onto the most durable character in the party. Damage output is low and not the point — Guardian's combat contribution is measured in how much the rest of the team survives to act.
