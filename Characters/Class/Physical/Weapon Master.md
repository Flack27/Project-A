The only physical class that can access all weapons. Class skills draw from a single curated Weapon Master pool split into sub-pools associated with the three weapon categories. Weapon developed before 3 star skews the S3 and S4 draws toward that category's sub-pool without locking it — piercing weapons skew toward speed and crit-adjacent skills, slashing toward AoE and offensive options, bludgeoning toward CC and DEF-focused skills. All skills in the pool are designed for broad utility rather than niche class synergy, making any draw functional regardless of build direction. Ceiling in any one direction is lower than a dedicated specialist — the trade is flexibility and unpredictability over raw optimisation.
