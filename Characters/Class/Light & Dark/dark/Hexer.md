Dark support built around two mirror skills. Divergence strips enemy buffs and converts them into debuffs before spreading the full debuff state of the primary target across all enemies — Hexer-applied debuffs only. Convergence strips ally debuffs and inverts them into corresponding buffs, healing the team for each debuff converted. HP investment drives both HP Siphon's damage output and its healing return. Action economy is the secondary identity — Cursed Cadence drains enemy turn meter through sustained debuff application and deepens debuff uptime on every class skill use. Distinct from Harrower which pressures through debuff detonation and damage scaling — Hexer's contribution is manipulation, turning the opponent's debuff gameplan against them and converting ally suffering into strength.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Divergence** — Single target active. Convert each buff into a corresponding debuff applied to that enemy. Spread all debuffs currently on the target to all enemies.

**Convergence** — AoE active. Convert each debuff into a corresponding buff applied to that ally. Heal all allies for a fixed amount per debuff converted.

**Siphon** — AoE active. Deal damage to all enemies equal to Hexer's max HP. Steal turn meter from all enemies and distribute evenly to all allies. Heal all allies for the total damage dealt.

**Malediction** — AoE active. Apply [DEF-], [Unhealable] and [Oblivion] to all enemies.

**Cursed Cadence** — Passive. Each time Hexer applies a debuff to an enemy, chance to apply [Turn Meter-] to that enemy. Each class skill hit extends the duration of all debuffs on the target(s). Extension applies to Convergence.

---

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Dark Harvest** — When any ally receives a debuff, Hexer gains a small amount of turn meter. Triggers once per turn per ally.

**Burden of Many** — Reduce all damage taken by a fixed percentage for each unique debuff currently on enemies. Caps at a fixed number of stacks.

**Fracture** — Base application chance for all debuffs increases by a small fixed amount for each debuff already on the target. Caps at a fixed threshold.

**Scourge** — When an enemy is cleansed, deal a small amount of damage to that enemy and strip one buff. Triggers once per turn.
