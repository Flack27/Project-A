Dedicated light healer with access to the strongest revival and protection tools in the game. Resurrection applied proactively prevents death outright rather than reversing it — distinct from standard revive and unavailable to any non-light class. Sanctuary removes a target from combat entirely for a duration, enabling plays no physical support can replicate. Primary combat contribution is keeping allies alive through sustained healing, cleanse and preemptive protection rather than any offensive presence. The scarcest dedicated healer in the roster given LD awakening rarity.
