Dedicated light healer with the deepest recovery toolkit in the roster. Healing and shields scale with max HP. Incidental damage on skills that hit enemies scales with ATK but is not a meaningful damage source and not a gearing target. Primary combat contribution is sustained team survivability through healing, cleanse, buff extension and proactive protection. The only class with access to both AoE immunity and revive, making her the definitive answer to debuff-heavy and burst-heavy teams respectively. Purely supportive — no offensive identity, no selfish mechanics.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Absolution** — AoE active. Extend all buffs on all allies by 1 turn. Reduce all debuffs on all allies by 2 turns. Reduce all skill cooldowns on all allies by 1 turn. Value scales with how many buffs and debuffs are currently active — strongest when used mid-fight rather than preemptively.

**Deliverance** — Single target active. Two modes depending on target state. If the target is alive: restore a large amount of HP scaling with Cleric's max HP and apply [Immunity]. If the target is dead: revive at a set HP threshold and apply [Invincibility]. Dead target mode has a significantly longer cooldown than the alive mode — the skill's CD on use is determined by which mode fired.

**Divine Aegis** — AoE active. Remove all debuffs from all allies. Apply [Immunity] to all allies.

**Exaltation** — AoE active. Apply [ATK+], [Heal Over Time] and [ACC+] to all allies. Extend all buffs currently on all allies by 1 turn after application, including the buffs just applied.

**Benediction** — Passive. Basic attacks heal the two lowest HP allies for a fixed amount scaling with Cleric's max HP. Basic attacks also apply a small [Turn Meter+] to self.

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Restoration** — At the start of Cleric's turn, restore HP to all allies equal to a fixed percentage of each ally's max HP.

**Renewal** — At the start of each ally's turn, a fixed chance to reduce one random skill cooldown on that ally by 1 turn.

**Light's Embrace** — Each unique buff active across all allies reduces all damage taken by Cleric by a fixed percentage. Caps at a fixed number of unique buffs.

**Mending Light** — Whenever an ally triggers a follow-up or extra attack, Cleric heals that ally for a fixed amount scaling with Cleric's max HP.
