High crit burst dealer with persistent stealth cycling as the primary survival tool. Ambush gives stealth uptime from the first turn, refreshing every other turn independently of skill usage — the class does not need to spend actions to stay protected. Shroud is the burst setup moment, applying Crit DMG+ and granting an extra turn from stealth to act twice before the enemy can respond. Expose and Vanish handle the enemy team's buffed states and return the class to stealth after disruption. Predator spreads bleed across all hits and rewards focusing a single target down, compounding with the crit focus through Exposed. Distinct from Rogue which strips and debuffs broadly from a single stealth window — Assassin Weapon Master maintains stealth as a persistent cycling state and uses it to control when and how it commits to a fight.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Preparation** — Single target active. Apply [ATK+] to self, then immediately deal high single target damage to the target.

**Predator** — Passive. Bonus damage against Bleeding targets, scaling with the number of Bleed stacks currently on them. All skills have a base chance to apply [Bleed] on hit.

**Expose** — AoE active. [Strip] one buff from all enemies. Apply [DEF-] and [Block] to all enemies. Strip chance and debuff application chance are both increased when the triggering hit crits.

**Vanish** — AoE active. [Strip] one buff from all enemies. Apply [ACC-] to all enemies. Application chance is increased when the triggering hit crits. Apply [Stealth] to self after the skill resolves.

**Shroud** — Passive. Combat begins with [Stealth] applied to self. Refreshes at the start of every other turn. (unique)

**Undercover** — Active. Apply [Turn Meter+] to all allies. Apply [Stealth] to self. (unique)

---

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Razor's Edge** — Increase crit rate by a flat amount.

**Quarry** — At the start of each turn, designate the lowest HP enemy. Deal bonus damage to that target this turn.

**Wide Open** — Bonus damage against targets with no active buffs. (unique)

**Killing Spree** — When an enemy dies, reset own skill cooldowns.

**Slaughter** — Gain [Turn Meter+] when this character kills an enemy.

**From the Shadows** — Bonus damage on all hits while [Stealth] is active. Only appears in the minor draw pool if any stealth skill already occupies a slot on this character.
