Offensive frontline that converts DEF investment into control and damage amplification. Two AoE skills disrupt the enemy team simultaneously — Crushing Wave lands CC and DEF reduction while Dominance pulls action bars and suppresses crit output. Exploit spreads Vulnerable across all targets from every hit, the proc chance scaling with DEF so the same investment that keeps this character alive determines how frequently the whole team's damage is amplified. Threshold absorbs burst that would otherwise bypass the control gameplan. Threatening Blow and Pressure Point handle single target pressure. Distinct from Warden which cycles threat and generates offense through counters — Bruiser Weapon Master does not rely on being attacked, it disrupts and degrades the enemy team directly.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Threshold** — Passive. When a single hit would deal damage exceeding a fixed percentage of max HP, the excess damage is reduced by a fixed percentage. Scales linearly with HP investment.

**Crushing Wave** — AoE active. Deal X% damage to all enemies. Apply [Stun] and [DEF-] to all enemies hit.

**Supremacy** — AoE active. Pull all enemies' action bars back by X%. Apply [CRIT Rate-] to all enemies. Apply a DEF-scaling [Shield] to self after the skill resolves. (unique)

**Devastating Blow** — Single target active. Deal X% damage. Apply [DEF-] to the target. (unique)

**Exploit** — Passive. All skills have a percentage chance to apply [Vulnerable] to their target, scaling with total DEF. The bonus damage [Vulnerable] applies is increased by X% for damage dealt by this character.

**Pressure Point** — Single target active. Apply [Vulnerable] and [DEF-] to the target.

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Tempered** — Increase DEF by a fixed amount.

**Accumulation** — Each skill use grants a stacking DEF buff. Stacks up to a fixed cap and persist until combat ends. (unique)

**Battle Rhythm** — Every 2 turns, push all allies' action bars forward by X%.

**Ironhide** — The first skill hit received each turn deals reduced damage.

**Razor's Edge** — Increase crit rate by a flat amount.

**Reprisal** — Grants a flat counter chance on incoming hits. Stacks additively with other counter sources.
