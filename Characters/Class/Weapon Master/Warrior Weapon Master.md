ATK-scaling aggressor that sustains itself through kills and damage dealt rather than defensive investment. Press the Attack and War Shout open the enemy team, Finishing Blow and Flurry convert weakened targets into kills, Killing Blow extends the chain when kills land. Bloodprice and Carnage create a self-sustaining loop — spending HP to deal damage generates a shield, and killing enemies generates further shields, meaning aggressive forward play keeps the character alive rather than exposing it. Indomitable is the safety net that resets a fight gone wrong back into a winnable position. Distinct from Berserker which scales from low HP and leans into the damage it takes — Warrior Weapon Master wants to stay at full strength and uses the kill chain to stay there.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Finishing Blow** — Single target active. Multi-hit skill. The final hit deals bonus damage equal to a percentage of the target's missing HP. Damage scales with how low the target is — the closer to death, the harder the last hit lands.

**Press the Attack** — AoE active. Deal X% damage to all enemies. The main target takes bonus damage on top of the AoE hit.

**Restitution** — Active. Consumes a reduced percentage of current HP compared to the base version. Deals ATK-scaling bonus damage proportional to HP spent. Converts the HP consumed into a shield applied to self for the duration. (unique)

**Flurry** — Active. Designate 3 basic attacks distributed freely across any enemies. Attacks can be focused on one target or spread.

**Dauntless** — Passive. Once per combat, when a hit would reduce this character to 0 HP a large shield is applied to self instead. Shield value is fixed and significant — large enough to absorb follow-up hits and continue fighting. Cooldown begins after activation. (unique)

**Decisive Strike** — Single target active. High damage hit. If this skill kills the target gain an extra turn else apply [Shield] to self. (unique)

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Crippling Shot** — Basic attacks have a chance to apply a minor [Turn Meter-] to the target.

**Predator** — Increase ATK by a fixed amount.

**Bloodbath** — Each enemy killed applies a stacking shield to self. (unique)

**Bloodthirst** — Heal for a fixed percentage of damage dealt by basic attacks and single target skills.

**Piercing Edge** — Basic attacks ignore a portion of target DEF.

**Death Knell** — When any character dies, automatically trigger S1 weapon skill against the lowest HP enemy. Has an internal cooldown.
