Frontline tank that converts HP investment into team protection and grows more durable as allies take punishment. Absorb and Bloodward create a self-reinforcing identity — taking damage on behalf of allies and spending HP to shield them means the same stat drives both survival and utility output. Iron Constitution stacks max HP as the fight progresses, compounding Bloodward's shield value and Absorb's recovery threshold the longer the team stays under pressure. Brace softens debuff uptime across the whole team rather than removing it outright, providing relief without the binary dependency on cleanse timing. Distinct from Guardian which intercepts and redirects damage actively — Tank Weapon Master absorbs it passively and builds from it. Distinct from Bruiser which converts HP into damage output. Broadest weapon access of any physical class, reflecting mastery across disciplines rather than depth in one.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Shield Wall** — AoE active. Apply [Taunt] and [ATK-] to all enemies. Apply a HP-scaling [Shield] to self.

**Absorb** — Passive. Takes 25% of damage directed at other teammates. For every X% max HP lost this way, gain a stack. At X stacks, consume them to recover a percentage of max HP.

**Bloodward** — Active. Consumes a percentage of current HP. Converts that value into a shield applied to all allies and applies [Heal Over Time] to the team for the duration.

**Brace** — AoE active. Decrease the duration of all debuffs on all allies by 1 turn. Apply [DEF+] to all allies. (unique)

**Iron Constitution** — Passive. Gain a stacking max HP buff each time an ally takes damage from an enemy action. Stacks once per enemy action regardless of how many allies are hit — AoE attacks grant one stack. Cap at 10 stacks. Stacks are lost at the end of combat. (unique)

**Vanguard Call** — Active. Apply [Turn Meter+] and [Shield] to all allies. (unique)

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Resilience** — Reduce all incoming damage by a fixed percentage.

**Cover** — Passive with cooldown. When a hit that would kill an ally lands, Tank Weapon Master intercepts it instead, taking the full hit. Tank Weapon Master's DEF applies normally. Cooldown begins after activation.

**Resolve** — Increase max HP by a fixed amount.

**Provoke** — When this character takes damage, percentage chance to apply [Taunt] to the attacker.

**Unstoppable** — Cannot be reduced to 0 HP while affected by [Stun], [Sleep], [Entangle] or [Silence]. Cleanses crowd control at the start of the turn when below X% HP, on a cooldown.

**Steadfast** — While any enemy is Taunted or the WM is shielded, reduce all damage taken by a fixed percentage. (unique)
