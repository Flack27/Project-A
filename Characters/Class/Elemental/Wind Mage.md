Versatile elemental class built around SPD, action bar manipulation and HP scaling. The most flexible of the mage types — equally capable as a buffer, bleed debuffer or healer depending on investment direction. Lower ATK than fire mage but more durable and more team-oriented by default.

Requires wind elemental awakening. See [[Elemental Awakening]].

## Build Axes

**Buffer** — action bar manipulation, ATK up and SPD up application, keeping fast allies moving and slow allies relevant. The most team-dependent build, rewards knowing your roster well.

**Bleed debuffer** — bleed application scaling with target max HP rather than wind mage ATK, meaning low ATK is not a liability for this build. Accuracy investment amplifies bleed reliability. Pairs well with a consumer character who can burst the accumulated bleed stacks.

**HP scaling healer** — heals scaling with the wind mage's own HP rather than ATK. High + HP base makes this a meaningful healing output. More healing focused than fire mage, less than earth mage. Can mix healing and buffing naturally in the same build.

## Notes

Wind mage is the most team-dependent class in the mage family. A wind mage in isolation underperforms relative to their investment — their value compounds with the team around them. A fire mage can carry a fight alone, a wind mage makes everyone else carry harder.
