Dedicated healer built around HoT uptime as a persistent team state rather than reactive burst healing. Multiple skills apply HoT as a byproduct of their primary effect, feeding into Fertile Ground's duration extension and the damage reduction passive. Cleanse is woven into the kit rather than occupying a dedicated skill slot — the turn passive handles baseline debuff management, freeing active skills for layered healing and CC. Distinct from Cleric which is reactive and protection focused — Druid maintains a sustained healthy state across the whole team through consistent HoT cycling and proactive debuff management.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Entangling Roots** — AoE active. Apply [Entangle] to all enemies. Heal all allies for a fixed amount scaling with Druid's max HP.

**Nature's Cleanse** — AoE active. Remove all debuffs from all allies. Heal all allies for a fixed amount scaling with Druid's max HP. Apply [Heal Over Time] to all allies.

**Wither and Bloom** — AoE active. Apply [ATK-] and [ACC-] to all enemies. Apply [Immunity] to all allies.

**Sylvan Renewal** — AoE active. Apply [Heal Over Time], [DEF+] and [ACC+] to all allies.

**Natural Order** — Passive. At the start of each ally's turn, cleanse one debuff from that ally and heal them for a fixed amount scaling with Druid's max HP. If no debuff is present, apply [Heal Over Time] instead.

---

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Fertile Ground** — All [Heal Over Time] effects applied by Druid tick one additional time before expiring.

**Living Canopy** — Allies with an active [Heal Over Time] applied by Druid take reduced damage from all sources.

**Regrowth** — At the start of Druid's turn, a fixed chance to reduce one of Druid's skill cooldowns by 1 turn.

**Abundant Harvest** — Increase all healing done by Druid by a fixed percentage.
