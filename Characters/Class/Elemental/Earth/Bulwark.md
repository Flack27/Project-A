Frontline tank that generates ally utility as a byproduct of its own combat presence. Shields scale with max HP making HP the primary investment target — the same stat that keeps it alive also determines team mitigation output. Cleanse and heal over time emerge from its own actions rather than dedicated support turns, keeping the selfish playstyle intact while still contributing to team survivability. Distinct from Guardian which intercepts damage actively — Earth Melee keeps allies healthy and shielded proactively without redirecting threat onto itself. Distinct from Bruiser which converts HP investment into damage output. Low damage threat, combat contribution measured entirely in how long the team survives.
