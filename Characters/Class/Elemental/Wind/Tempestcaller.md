Tempo-focused support built around accelerating allies and disrupting enemy action economy simultaneously. SPD investment drives effect accuracy through the minor pool, meaning the same stat that makes Tempestcaller act more frequently also makes her debuffs land more reliably. Tailwind is a unique unremovable single-use buff that causes the recipient to assist on the next attack skill used by any team member, disappearing after the assist fires. The random passive applies Tailwind and Immunity together each turn, rewarding teams where every ally benefits from an assist rather than builds that depend on a specific recipient. No healing identity — sustain comes entirely from the CD manipulation skill's HP pulse.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Slipstream** — Single target active. Apply [Tailwind] and [Turn Meter+] to a selected ally. Apply [SPD+] to all allies.

**Stormfront** — AoE active. Apply [ACC+] to all allies. Apply [SPD-] and [Sleep] to all enemies.

**Cyclone** — AoE active. Apply [ATK+] to all allies. Apply [Silence] to all enemies.

**Monsoon** — AoE active. Increase all skill cooldowns on all enemies by 1 turn. Reduce all skill cooldowns on all allies by 1 turn. Heal all allies for a fixed percentage of their max HP.

**Windborne** — Passive. At the start of Tempestcaller's turn, apply [Tailwind] and [Immunity] to a random ally. Build teams where every ally benefits from a Tailwind assist — the random target is the identity, not a limitation.

---

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Firstwind** — At the start of combat, apply [Tailwind] to the ally with the highest ATK.

**Galeforce** — Effect accuracy increases with SPD at a fixed ratio, up to a cap. SPD investment improves both action frequency and debuff reliability simultaneously.

**Squall** — Whenever an enemy uses a skill, chance to apply a small [Turn Meter-] to that enemy.

**Zephyr** — All buffs applied by Tempestcaller last 1 additional turn.
