AoE-focused wind damage dealer built around turn meter manipulation and debuff pressure. Effect accuracy is the defining secondary stat — the major passive trades crit entirely for guaranteed DEF ignore scaling with EFF ACC, meaning no crit investment is needed and all resources consolidate into ATK and EFF ACC. Every skill touches turn meter or applies a debuff, making the passive minor pool feel active even between skill uses. Distinct from Galebreaker which is single target burst and strip — Aeromancer is sustained AoE pressure that controls the entire enemy team's action economy simultaneously.

---

## Major Pool

Class-defining skills and passives. Two are drawn across slots 3 and 4, with at least one always being an active.

**Cyclone Burst** — AoE active. Apply [Silence] and [Turn Meter-] to all enemies.

**Jet Stream** — AoE active. Apply [SPD+] to all allies. Deal AoE damage to all enemies applying [Turn Meter-]. Distribute the total turn meter removed across all allies as [Turn Meter+].

**Bloodwind** — AoE active. Strike all enemies four times. Each hit has an independent chance to apply [Bleed] to each target. The fourth hit has a chance to inflict [Silence]

**Eye of the Storm** — AoE active. Deal damage to all enemies. Chance to increase all enemy skill cooldowns by 1 turn. Reduce all of Aeromancer's skill cooldowns by 1 turn.

**Piercing Gale** — Passive. Aeromancer cannot critically hit. In exchange, a deal increased damage and a portion of DEF is ignored on all damage dealt, scaling with Aeromancer's effect accuracy up to a cap. 

---

## Minor Pool

Smaller refinements drawn at slots 5 and 6. Always passives.

**Windfall** — Chance to steal a fixed percentage of turn meter from the primary target on every class skill used.

**Windshear** — Basic attacks have a chance to apply [Silence] to the target.

**Updraft** — At the start of Aeromancer's turn, apply a small [Turn Meter+] to the ally with the lowest current turn meter.

**Momentum** — At the start of Aeromancer's turn, gain one stack of [SPD+] up to a maximum of 5 stacks. Each stack provides a small fixed SPD increase. Stacks persist for the duration of combat.
