Characters are the core of the game. Every system exists to develop them, every piece of content is cleared through them. No character is objectively better than another — a 1 star raised well can match a 4 star. Higher stars start more developed but the ceiling is accessible from anywhere with investment.

---

## What Makes a Character

**Race** — the species a character belongs to, carried from the shared world before the stasis. Influences elemental awakening probability, weapon tendency and carries a light racial identity. See [[Race]].

**Stats** — four primary stats that define combat capability: ATK, DEF, HP and SPD. Base values are set by race, variance is determined by growth rate. Secondary stats — crit rate, crit damage, effect accuracy, effect resistance — are built through gear and skills rather than the character themselves. See [[Stats]].

**Elemental Awakening** — at 2 star a character either awakens to an element or they do not. Awakening opens elemental class paths and the elemental passive pool, and grants a small built-in elemental passive. Characters without an awakening develop entirely through physical class options. See [[Elemental Awakening]].

**Class** — locks in at 3 star and defines combat identity from that point forward. Physical, elemental or light and dark depending on awakening status. Unlocks the first character skill and the defining class passive. See [[Characters/Class/Class]].

**Skills** — four active slots and passive slots unlocking through star progression. The first two active slots are weapon techniques, fully interchangeable. The third and fourth are class skills, permanently locked once learned. Passives draw from class and elemental pools. See [[Skills]].

**Traits** — things a character is or becomes through life in the lobby and the tower. Flat traits describe personality. Evolving traits describe vocation and role. Both feed into combat behaviour, lobby activity and auto-battle tendencies. Governed by a hidden aptitude value independent from growth. See [[Traits]].

**Memory Fragments** — forgotten moments from before the stasis surfacing as a character grows. Three fixed memories across 1 to 3 star, one shaped reflection at 6 star influenced by the world the player built. Skippable but rewarding. Seed traits and add personality context that carries into how the character plays. See [[Memory Fragments]].

## Hidden Values

Every character has two hidden values seeded at summon that govern how they develop.

**Growth** — combat development speed. Affects XP gain rate, stat threshold rewards and weapon technique progression. Visible in its effects over time, never shown directly.

**Aptitude** — trait development. Affects how readily traits unlock, how high their personal ceilings sit, and how likely ascension rolls are to open new trait slots.

Both are independent and both can increase through play. A character can surprise the player by growing into something unexpected. This is intended.

---

## Star Progression

Characters advance through star levels from 1 to 6, with 7 as an endgame ceiling. Each ascension raises the level cap, rolls for new trait slots, and unlocks new skills and passives at defined thresholds. Higher star characters summoned directly arrive with earlier progression already resolved. See [[Star Progression]].
