
A reference for what the tutorial needs to teach and roughly when. Implementation, dialogue and exact sequencing is designed later when systems are more finalised. Nothing here is a script, just a map of what needs to be covered before the player hits floor 10.

---

#### Must cover before first combat

- World context and Architect framing — brief, atmospheric
- Summoning — first pull, meeting starter characters
- Basic combat — turn order, skills, targeting
- Weapons — equipping, why they matter
- Death and recovery — ideally at first death naturally

#### Must cover floors 1-5

- Auto battle — introduction and limitations
- Traits — first trait unlock moment
- Expeditions — passive gathering, expedition slots
- Smithy — building it, basic crafting
- Weapon skills and technique system — when a character develops first technique
- Lobby basics — what buildings do, assignment

#### Must cover floors 5-10

- Active vs passive gathering distinction
- Weapon upgrading loop
- Morale — at first visible morale change
- Second summon and crystal economy briefly

#### Must cover at floor 10 clear

- Boss telegraphing — just before the fight
