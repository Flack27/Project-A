Gear crafting. Takes materials from expeditions, tower floors and dungeons and produces weapons and equipment. Basic smithy produces common gear and occasionally uncommon. Higher upgrade levels unlock better recipes and improve baseline output quality.

A character with a blacksmith vocation trait assigned here improves output quality and speed beyond what building upgrades alone can achieve. An unassigned smithy operates at baseline. Higher blacksmith tiers produce results the building alone cannot replicate regardless of upgrade level — the vocation matters as much as the building.

Upgrade path — output quality, crafting speed, recipe variety. Mid tier upgrades require materials from dungeon content.

Chance to upgrade to next tier not guaranteed, improved by smithy trait
