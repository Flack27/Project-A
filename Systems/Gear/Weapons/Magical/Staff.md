Staff is the offensive magic weapon. S1 is a fixed basic attack developing through three mastery levels with no specialisation. S2 has three options each developing through four mastery levels, the fourth being specialisation. The CD reduction option is locked until mastery level 4 is reached on either of the first two options.

The elemental rider applied by the character's awakening attaches to all staff techniques including the basic attack.

---

## S1 — Basic Attack

Single target magic strike. ATK scaling. Mastery increases damage at levels 2 and 3. No specialisation.

---

## S2

**Single Target** — concentrated strike, 3 hits. ATK scaling. Mastery increases damage per hit at levels 2 and 3.

_Specialisation_ — 4 hits at reduced damage per hit keeping roughly the same total, or 2 hits at increased damage per hit for a higher total.

---

**AoE** — magic burst hitting all enemies, 1 hit. ATK scaling. Mastery increases damage at levels 2 and 3.

_Specialisation_ — 2 hits at slightly reduced total damage, or 1 hit with a 1 turn cd reduction

---

**CD Reduction** — reduces all cooldowns on one target ally by 1 turn. No damage component.

_Specialisation_ — broadens to reduce all allies' cooldowns by 1 turn, or stays single target and reduces by 2 turns instead.
