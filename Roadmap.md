
#### Phase 5 — Content Structure

Targets:

- Tower.md — floor structure, milestone unlocks, repeat rewards, climbing rules
- Dungeons.md — dungeon categories, what each farms, energy and difficulty structure

#### Notes

Phase order is sequenced but not rigid — if a phase 3 question forces a phase 4 decision, take it. The point of breadth-first is to avoid the trap of perfecting one corner while the next corner reshapes it.

Loose Threads is the catch for anything that surfaces mid-phase and doesn't fit the current target. Don't chase tangents within a phase — log and continue.
